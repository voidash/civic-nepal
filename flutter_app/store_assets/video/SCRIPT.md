# Nagarik Patro - Video Script

## Overview
- **Total Duration:** ~60-75 seconds
- **Target:** Play Store promo / Social media
- **Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, empowering

---

## SCENE 1: Hook (0:00 - 0:05)
**Screen:** App icon + App launch
**Voiceover:**
> "Every Nepali needs this app on their phone."

---

## SCENE 2: Calendar - The Main Hook (0:05 - 0:15)
**Screen:** Calendar screen with dates, scroll through month
**Voiceover:**
> "Nagarik Patro gives you the complete Bikram Sambat calendar. Tithi, nakshatra, festivals — all in one place. Never miss an auspicious day for weddings, bratabandha, or pasni."

---

## SCENE 3: Date Converter (0:15 - 0:22)
**Screen:** Date converter - show BS to AD conversion
**Voiceover:**
> "Need to convert dates? Switch between Bikram Sambat and Gregorian instantly. Accurate and reliable."

---

## SCENE 4: Live Financial Data (0:22 - 0:35)
**Screen:** Forex rates → Gold prices → IPO screen
**Voiceover:**
> "Stay updated with live exchange rates from Nepal Rastra Bank. Check today's gold and silver prices. Track IPO openings and NEPSE stock prices — all updated daily."

---

## SCENE 5: Know Your Rights (0:35 - 0:47)
**Screen:** Constitution screen, scroll through rights categories, tap an article
**Voiceover:**
> "Know your rights as a Nepali citizen. The complete Constitution of Nepal — all 308 articles — in both Nepali and English. With practical explanations of your fundamental rights."

---

## SCENE 6: Interactive Maps (0:47 - 0:57)
**Screen:** District map, tap on a district, show local officials
**Voiceover:**
> "Explore Nepal's 77 districts. Tap any district to see your elected officials — mayors, deputy mayors, and ward representatives. Democracy at your fingertips."

---

## SCENE 7: Government & Services (0:57 - 1:05)
**Screen:** How Nepal Works screen, scroll through sections
**Voiceover:**
> "Understand how your government works. Current cabinet ministers, the law-making process, and documents you need for government services."

---

## SCENE 8: Utilities (1:05 - 1:12)
**Screen:** Quick show of citizenship merger and image compressor
**Voiceover:**
> "Plus useful tools — merge citizenship photos for applications, compress images for online forms."

---

## SCENE 9: Closing - Trust & CTA (1:12 - 1:20)
**Screen:** Back to home, show "No ads" message, then app icon + download button
**Voiceover:**
> "No ads. No tracking. Your data stays on your device. Download Nagarik Patro — free on Google Play."

---
