Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Nagarik ("the App") is a civic information app for Nepal. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

The App collects minimal data to function:

  • No personal information: We do not require registration, login, or any personal details.
  • No tracking: We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • Local storage only: Your preferences (language, theme) are stored locally on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • App settings (language preference, theme mode)
  • Cached images for offline access
  • Photos you select for the citizenship merger tool (processed locally, never uploaded)

Third-Party Services

The App fetches public data from:

  • Nepal Patro API: For forex rates and gold/silver prices (no personal data sent)
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates

We do not share any data with advertisers or third-party analytics providers.

Permissions

The App may request:

  • Camera/Photos: Only for the citizenship photo merger tool. Photos are processed locally and never uploaded.
  • Internet: To fetch live forex/bullion rates and check for updates.
  • Notifications: Optional, for calendar reminders (if enabled).

Data Security

All data processing happens locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The content is educational and suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
